Delete Your Mystica Account

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including palm reading history, saved readings, and account information. This action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Mystica app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Look for "Delete Account" or "Account Settings"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

If you cannot delete your account through the app, please contact us:

  • Email: gcpaps2@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Delete My Mystica Account"
  • Include: The email address associated with your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (email, profile)
  • All saved palm readings and history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any stored images or photos

Data Retention

Once you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymous usage statistics may be retained for analytics
  • Backups containing your data will be purged within 90 days

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or data removal, contact us at:

Email: gcpaps2@gmail.com
App: Mystica - Tarot

Alternative: Temporary Deactivation

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, you can:

  • Log out of the app
  • Uninstall the app (your data remains for when you return)
  • Contact us about temporary account suspension

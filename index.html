Privacy Policy for Mystica

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mystica ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our palm reading application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Data

Our app uses your device's camera to capture images of your palm for palm reading analysis. These images are:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Used solely for palm reading interpretation
  • Not stored permanently unless you choose to save readings
  • Never shared with third parties

2.2 Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Basic profile information
  • Reading history (if you choose to save readings)

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide palm reading services
  • Maintain your account and reading history
  • Improve our app functionality
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is encrypted during transmission
  • We use secure cloud services (Firebase) for data storage
  • Camera images are processed locally when possible
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered palm reading interpretations

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

6. Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture palm images for readings
  • Internet: To sync data and provide AI interpretations
  • Network State: To check connectivity

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
